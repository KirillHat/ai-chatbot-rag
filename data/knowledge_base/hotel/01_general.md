# Hotel Aurora — General Information

## About us

Hotel Aurora is a 24-room boutique hotel in the Old Town district. We opened
in 2018 in a fully renovated 1903 townhouse and have been independently owned
ever since. The hotel has 4 floors and one rooftop level.

## Location

- **Address:** 12 Old Mill Street, Old Town, 1010
- **Distance to the central square:** 280 m (3-minute walk)
- **Distance to the cathedral:** 450 m (5-minute walk)
- **Distance to the harbour:** 180 m (2-minute walk)
- **Distance to the airport:** 11 km (15–20 minutes by taxi, ~€18)
- **Nearest tram stop:** "Old Mill" (line 4), 70 m from the front door
- **Nearest metro station:** "Cathedral" (M1), 6 minutes on foot

## Check-in and check-out

- **Standard check-in:** from 15:00
- **Standard check-out:** by 11:00
- **Early check-in:** subject to availability; usually possible from 12:00 for an extra €25
- **Late check-out:** until 14:00 if available, €30 surcharge; until 18:00 charged as half day
- **Luggage storage:** complimentary, before check-in and after check-out
- **ID at check-in:** government-issued photo ID required for the lead guest
- **Payment hold:** we pre-authorise €50 per night on a credit card for incidentals; refunded within 7 days of check-out
