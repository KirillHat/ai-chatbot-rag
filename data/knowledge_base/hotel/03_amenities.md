# Amenities & Services

## Breakfast
- **Hours:** 07:00 – 10:30 daily, 08:00 – 11:00 on Sundays and bank holidays
- **Where:** Aurora dining room, ground floor
- **Style:** hot Mediterranean buffet — eggs cooked to order, charcuterie, cheeses, fresh fruit, pastries from a local bakery
- **Coffee:** locally-roasted espresso, single-origin filter, full milk-based menu
- **Diets:** gluten-free, vegan, kosher and halal options available — please flag at booking or to the front desk the night before
- **Children:** under 6 eat free, 6–12 half price
- **Room service breakfast:** +€8 surcharge, must be ordered the night before

## Spa & wellness
- **Hours:** 07:00 – 22:00 daily; last entry 21:30
- **Facilities:** indoor heated pool (28°C), Finnish sauna, steam room, two showers
- **Pool length:** 12 m, depth 1.2 m throughout — not lap-swim sized
- **What's included:** towels, robes for guests over 12, complimentary water and tea
- **Treatments:** massages and facials by appointment, weekday rates from €70 / 50 minutes
- **Children:** welcome in the pool until 19:00 if accompanied by an adult; the sauna is over-16 only

## Parking
- **Type:** underground garage with valet service
- **Price:** €18/night, charged separately at check-out
- **Capacity:** 12 spaces — book at the time of reservation
- **Height clearance:** 2.0 m (no SUVs over that height)
- **EV charging:** 2 chargers (Type 2, 22 kW), free for guests
- **Street parking:** metered until 19:00, free thereafter and on Sundays

## Wi-Fi
- **Network name:** Aurora-Guest (open) or Aurora-Secure (per-room password printed on the welcome card)
- **Speed:** 500 Mbps symmetric in every room, no data cap
- **Password help:** call the reception extension 9 from the in-room phone

## Pets
- **Allowed in:** Classic Double and Junior Suite categories only
- **Size:** dogs up to 25 kg and cats welcome
- **Surcharge:** first pet free, second pet €15/night
- **What's provided:** a bowl, a blanket and a treat on arrival — you'll need to bring your own food
- **House rules:** pets cannot be left alone in the room; the spa, dining room and bar are pet-free

## Transfers
- **Airport transfer:** €35 one-way for up to 3 passengers, book 12 h ahead
- **Train station transfer:** €15 one-way
- **Bicycle rental:** €15/day, helmets included, paid at the front desk
