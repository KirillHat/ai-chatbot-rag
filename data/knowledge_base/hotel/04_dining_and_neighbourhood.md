## Walking tour suggestions
- **Old Town loop** (1.5 hours): central square → cathedral → harbour promenade → back to the hotel via the artisan market. Best in the early morning before crowds.
- **Sunset over the harbour** (45 minutes): walk to the lighthouse for sundown — about 25 minutes each way along the seawall. The bar at the end is touristy; skip it.
- **Local market** (Saturdays only): 09:00 – 13:00 in the central square, 7 minutes on foot.

## Practical tips
- **Currency:** euros; ATMs on the central square and at the harbour. Cards accepted nearly everywhere; tipping 5–10% is enough.
- **Tap water:** safe to drink, mineral content is high
- **Pharmacies:** the closest is open 09:00 – 22:00 daily, on the corner of Old Mill and Cathedral Streets
- **Emergency:** dial 112 for police, fire and ambulance
