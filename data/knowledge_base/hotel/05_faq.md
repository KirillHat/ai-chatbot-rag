# Frequently Asked Questions

### Do you have family rooms?
We don't have a dedicated family category, but Junior Suites and Terrace
Suites both accommodate two adults plus two children comfortably. A
roll-away bed is +€35/night; cribs are free.

### Can I get a quiet room away from the street?
Yes. Classic Doubles all face the inner courtyard and are by far the
quietest in the building. If you book a Deluxe Sea View and want quiet,
ask the reception to move you up a floor — the third- and fourth-floor
rooms are notably quieter than the first.

### Do you have rooms accessible for guests with reduced mobility?
We have one Classic Double on the ground floor with a roll-in shower, grab
rails and a 90 cm doorway. The lift reaches all guest floors and the spa.
The rooftop terrace is reached by a short flight of stairs and is not
step-free.

### Is there air conditioning?
Yes — every room has individually-controlled AC and underfloor heating
in the bathroom.

### Can I extend my stay once I'm here?
If we have availability, yes — same room if possible, otherwise we move
you. Tell the front desk before 10:00 of your departure day. The same
rate you booked at applies; no upgrade fee for moving categories if it's
the only option we can offer.

### Do you offer long-stay rates?
Stays of 7 nights or more on direct bookings get 15% off. Stays of 28
nights or more are quoted manually — email reservations.

### Can I store luggage after check-out?
Yes, free. Up to 24 hours in the locked luggage room behind reception.

### Is breakfast really included?
Yes, in every published rate on our website. Third-party booking sites
sometimes display "room only" rates; on those we charge €18/person/day for
breakfast, payable at check-in.

### Can I get a late check-out for a flight that night?
We try, but it depends on housekeeping. Until 14:00 is usually fine for
+€30; later than that we'll offer luggage storage and use of the spa
showers and lounge instead.

### Do you charge a city tax?
Yes — €2/person/night, automatically included in the rates we quote on
our site. Children under 12 are exempt.

### Can I check in if I'm under 18?
We can only check in guests aged 18 or older. Under-18 guests must be
accompanied by a parent or legal guardian.

### Do you take group bookings?
Up to 8 rooms is straightforward — email us. Above 8 rooms we ask for a
non-refundable 30% deposit.

### Can I get a quote for hosting a small event?
The dining room can be private-hired for events from 16:00 onwards.
Capacity 30 seated / 45 standing. Email events@hotel-aurora.example for a
proposal.
