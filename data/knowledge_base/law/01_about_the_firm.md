# About Bennett & Klein

## The firm

Bennett & Klein is a boutique business and intellectual-property law firm
based in Boston, Massachusetts. We were founded in 2014 by Sarah Bennett
(formerly at Wilson Sonsini) and David Klein (formerly at Goodwin Procter)
to advise early- and growth-stage technology companies.

We are five attorneys and two paralegals. We pick our clients carefully
and decline more than half of incoming work — usually because it falls
outside our practice areas, not because of capacity.

## Who we work with best

- US-incorporated startups (Delaware C-Corp, LLC) at seed through Series B
- Non-US founders setting up a US entity for the first time
- B2B SaaS companies with US customers
- Solo creators, agencies and small studios with revenue under $5M

## Who we are not the right fit for

- Personal injury, family law, criminal defense, immigration — we don't practise these.
- Large public-company M&A — we'll refer you to a peer firm.
- Anything urgent in litigation. We are transactional lawyers.
- Legal questions that need an answer in less than 24 hours — we book all
  consultations for the next business day at the earliest.
