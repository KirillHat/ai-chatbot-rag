# Practice Areas

## Commercial Contracts

We draft, review and negotiate the contracts that early-stage tech
companies actually need:

- **Mutual & one-way NDAs** — review or draft from a template
- **Master Services Agreements (MSA)** and statements of work
- **SaaS Subscription Agreements** and order forms
- **Customer Terms of Service** and Privacy Policies
- **Vendor agreements**, including data processing addenda (DPAs)
- **Independent contractor agreements** with proper IP assignment
- **Founder agreements** and equity vesting

We don't take on procurement-heavy enterprise sourcing or government
contracts.

## Intellectual Property

- **Trademarks** — clearance search, USPTO filing, prosecution, renewals
- **Copyright** — registration, licensing, DMCA takedowns
- **Trade secrets** — internal program design, NDAs, employee policies
- **IP assignment** for founders coming from a previous employer
- **Open-source compliance** — clearing AGPL/GPL/Apache concerns before fundraising

We do not handle patent prosecution. We refer patent work to two
specialty firms we trust.

## Cross-Border Matters

- **Forming a Delaware C-Corp** as a non-US founder
- **EOR and PEO** structures for hiring globally
- **EU/US data flows** — assessing reliance on the EU-US Data Privacy
  Framework, drafting SCCs, building a basic GDPR record
- **Going-to-market** in the US: contract templates, tax registrations,
  state-level qualifications

We are not tax advisors. We work alongside your accountants for tax
matters.

## Disputes & Compliance

- **Demand letters** and pre-litigation negotiation
- **GDPR/CCPA reviews** and remediation plans
- **Employment compliance** — handbooks, classification, wage notices
- **SOC 2 readiness counsel** — legal pieces only; we don't do the audit

We file lawsuits in Massachusetts state and federal courts when necessary,
but we are not a general litigation firm. Complex litigation is referred
out.

## What we politely decline

- Personal injury, divorce, criminal defense
- Real-estate transactions
- Tax planning or tax controversy
- Immigration filings
- Anything outside US/EU jurisdictions
