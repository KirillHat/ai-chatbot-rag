# Pricing & Intake Process

## How we bill

We try to quote a fixed fee for as much work as possible. When fixed
fees aren't realistic (negotiations, ongoing counsel), we bill by the
hour with a written cap.

| Service | Fee |
|---------|-----|
| Initial consultation (45 minutes, video) | $250 |
| NDA review — standard mutual | $400 fixed |
| NDA review — one-way / non-standard | $500 – $750 fixed |
| Customer SaaS Terms of Service drafting | from $1,800 |
| MSA + SOW drafting | from $3,500 |
| US Delaware C-Corp formation (founder package) | $1,500 + state fees |
| US trademark filing (one class, one mark) | $950 + USPTO filing fees ($350) |
| Trademark clearance search (US) | $400 |
| Open-source license audit | from $2,500 |
| GDPR / CCPA compliance review | from $4,500 |
| Hourly rate (when fixed fees don't fit) | $475 / hour |

Numbers are USD, exclusive of state fees and pass-through costs. Quoted
fees are firm — we don't issue surprise invoices.

## Retainers

For ongoing fractional general-counsel work, we offer monthly retainers
starting at $4,000 / month for up to 8 hours of work. Unused hours roll
over for one month. No long-term commitment.

## Payment

- Wire transfer or US ACH preferred
- Credit cards accepted up to $5,000 (3% surcharge)
- All fees due within 14 days of invoice
- 50% deposit on engagements over $5,000

## Intake process — four steps

### 1. Online intake form
Two minutes. We ask for your name, your company, the jurisdictions you
operate in, and a one-paragraph description of the work. Confidential.

### 2. Conflict check & confirmation
Within one business day, we run a conflict check against current and
recent clients. If we're cleared, we send you a Calendly link for the
consultation. If we're not, we tell you immediately and decline.

### 3. 45-minute consultation ($250, video)
We listen, ask questions, and tell you the legal landscape — even if
that means telling you that you don't need a lawyer for what you came
in for. By the end of the call we either:

- Quote a fixed fee for the work, or
- Tell you we're not the right fit and refer you elsewhere

### 4. Engagement letter
A one-page letter that spells out the scope, the deliverables, the fee
and the timeline. Once signed, the attorney-client relationship begins
and work starts within 3 business days unless agreed otherwise.

## Response times

- New client emails: under 1 business day
- Existing client emails: under 4 business hours during office hours
- Urgent matters (existing clients only): same-day response by phone
- We don't take pager calls overnight or on weekends
