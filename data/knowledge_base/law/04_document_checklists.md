# Document Checklists

For each engagement, send the following before the kick-off call. We
won't ask twice.

## NDA review (mutual or one-way)

- The draft NDA itself (Word .docx if possible, PDF acceptable)
- One sentence on who the counterparty is and what you're discussing
- Whether either party has an existing template you'd prefer to start from

## SaaS Terms of Service drafting

- Product description (2 paragraphs is fine — we'll ask follow-up questions)
- Subscription model: monthly, annual, freemium, usage-based
- Free trial: yes/no, length
- Hosting region(s) and any subprocessors you use (AWS, Azure, GCP, OpenAI, etc.)
- Whether you sell to consumers, businesses, or both
- Whether you collect any personal data and from which jurisdictions

## Delaware C-Corp formation

- Founder names, addresses and citizenships
- Company name (we'll check Delaware availability)
- Initial cap-table split between founders, with vesting expectations
- Planned business activity in 1–2 sentences
- Whether you'll need a US bank account (we partner with Mercury and Brex referrals; we're not paid for this)
- Whether you're based outside the US (changes the EIN process)

## US trademark filing

- The exact mark (word, logo, or both)
- Your goods/services in plain English (we translate to USPTO classes)
- Specimens of use (screenshots, marketing material) if you're already using the mark
- A short list of competitor brands you'd like us to clear against in the search

## GDPR / CCPA review

- Customer journey from sign-up to churn (a Loom video is great)
- Current Privacy Policy and Terms (URL is fine)
- List of subprocessors and which jurisdiction each is in
- Description of any AI features that use customer data for inference or training
- Whether you've ever received a Subject Access Request, deletion request, or regulator inquiry

## Things we don't need

- Your tax returns
- Your funding pitch deck (unless the engagement is fundraising-related)
- Personal data for anyone outside the engagement
- "Just a quick read" of your latest contract — we'll quote it as a real review
