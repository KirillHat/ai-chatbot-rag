AI Chatbot with RAG — live demos

Two industry demos that share one FastAPI + Claude + ChromaDB backend. Each landing loads the same embeddable widget but is rebranded and pointed at its own knowledge base.

Hospitality

Hotel Aurora — Concierge Bot

Boutique hotel landing. The bot answers questions on rooms, breakfast, check-in, parking, the spa and local recommendations.

 Legal

Bennett & Klein — Legal Assistant

Law-firm landing. The bot answers questions on practice areas, consultation pricing, document checklists, NDAs and intake.

 Admin

Knowledge-Base Admin

Upload a PDF or Markdown file → chunks are embedded into Chroma → the bot answers from it on the next message. No restart, no rebuild.
